Once this person has thrown it away it will lie there for years as these plastics can take up to 450 years to decompose. This does get rid of them but has many negative side effects that will and are causing detrimental effects to our climate. When these plastics are burned they release many toxics fumes that will not only damage us if someone were to breathe these fumes in but also they will rise up into our atmosphere and they will spread all over the world. Other chemicals are also released from cases being burned such as dioxins which will rise up into the atmosphere heating up our sky and the result of this is climate change around the whole world. This causes acid rain to be created which these plants need and instead of getting the water they need they are getting harmful chemicals that will eventually kill off these plants. Some reasons that could help stop this pandemic of plastic that is rising every year could be: Constructing more recycling plants. If more plants were built for people to drop off their old phone cases and plastic waste then the rate of these cases being dumped anywhere will drop.
